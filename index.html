Tribute to my brother

One of my biggest influences

He was very clever, friendly and very funny. The time we spent together we were always fighthing for silly things but we are protecting each other. He graduated in school with high grades and later he become a lawyer. However, his plans were cut short when he was initially diagnostic with Chron's disease in 2019 and was first diagnostic with skin cancer in 2016. During his 3 years remission the cancer return stronger and he died in 2019.

His legacy

  • Look after me since we lost our father
  • As a lawyer he was able to help people
  • Wrote a book with stories about depression

    • On my wedding day he wrote that to me: She doesn't fight with us because she love us, she fight for us because she loves us. Because that how she is, a lioness with those she love.
